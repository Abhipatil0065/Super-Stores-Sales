Super Store Sales Dashboard
1. Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data across multiple categories, regions, and customer segments. However, they often struggle to:

Track overall sales and profit performance
Identify high-performing product categories
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Analyze sales trends over time
Optimize shipping and discount strategies

Without proper analysis, businesses may face reduced profitability and inefficient decision-making.

2. Goal of the Dashboard

The main objective of this dashboard is to:

Monitor total sales, profit, and quantity sold
Identify top-performing product categories and products
Analyze yearly sales trends
Understand customer segments and their contribution
Evaluate shipping modes and order distribution
Enable data-driven business decisions

3. Key Visuals Used in Dashboard
KPI Cards

Used for:

Total Sales
Total Profit
Sold Quantity
Total Customers
Discount Given

Why used:
KPI cards provide a quick snapshot of key business performance indicators.

Category Highlight Cards

Used for:

Phones (Max Sales)
Copiers (Max Profit)
Binders (Max Quantity Sold)

Why used:
Highlights top-performing products for quick business insights.

Bar Chart

Used for:

Sales by Category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)

Why used:
Bar charts are ideal for comparing sales across categories.

Line Chart

Used for:

Sales by Year (2014–2017)

Why used:
Line charts show trends over time and help identify growth patterns.

Donut Chart

Used for:

Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

Why used:
Shows percentage contribution of each customer segment.

Table / Matrix

Used for:

Ship Mode vs Count of Orders

Why used:
Displays detailed comparison of shipping methods and order distribution.

Slicer (Filter Panel)

Used for:

State selection
Year selection

Why used:
Allows users to interactively filter data and perform dynamic analysis.

4. Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses to:

Identify high-revenue and high-profit product categories
Optimize pricing and discount strategies
Improve logistics by analyzing shipping modes
Understand customer segments for targeted marketing
Track sales growth and performance trends
Make faster and data-driven decisions
5. Key Insights Derived from Dashboard
Insight 1

Total sales reached 2.28M, with a total profit of 280.61K, indicating strong revenue generation.

Insight 2

Technology category contributes the highest sales (~0.83M), making it the most valuable segment.

Insight 3

Sales show consistent growth from 2015 to 2017, indicating business expansion.

Insight 4

Standard Class shipping has the highest number of orders, showing customer preference for cost-effective delivery.

Insight 5

Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales (~50%), making it the primary customer base.

Insight 6

Certain products:

Phones generate maximum sales
Copiers generate maximum profit
Binders have the highest quantity sold
